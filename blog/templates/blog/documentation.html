Creating Posts

Step-by-Step Guide

1. Access Editor

Click "New Post" from your dashboard or the navigation menu to access the post editor.

2. Write Content

Add a compelling title, write your content, and include an engaging excerpt.

3. Add Media

Upload a featured image to make your post visually appealing and shareable.

4. Add Tags

Use relevant tags to help readers discover your content through topic-based searches.

Post Status Options
Published Visible to all readers immediately
Draft Only visible to you for editing

Managing Posts

Dashboard Overview

Monitor your post performance, view statistics, and access all your content from one place.

Edit Posts

Update your content, change status, or modify tags at any time from the post edit page.

Delete Posts

Remove posts permanently. This action cannot be undone, so proceed with caution.

Note: Only post authors can edit or delete their own content. This ensures content ownership and security.
